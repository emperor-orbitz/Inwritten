                  Shirly@Cloudinary Mod Jordan Junior • 3 years ago • edited

                  Hey,
                  Each newly created asset, original upload or derived, is being counted towards your Transformations quota. For more information:
                  http://support.cloudinary.c...

                  Therefore, if you resize your image on the fly (e.g w_200) it count as a new transformation.

                  We kindly ask you to stay in the limit and if needed to upgrade your plan.

                  Also note that monthly quotas (i.e. Transformations and Bandwidth) are being counted within a last-30-days rolling window, therefore it never actually reset but updates every day.
                  Hope that helps,
